A fascination with stories

Welcome! I am Newt, a strange creature searching for meaning in a life defined by meaninglessness. I've been fascinated by stories of all kinds, and use them as a way to derive meaning. I started life as a programmer, but have since turned into more of a researcher and writer. While I'm fascinated by stories of all kinds and mediums, I am trying to teach myself the art of adaptation rather than creation. My interest in how these tales mutate with each retelling, and as such I do a lot of writing on established works.
